Mission Statement

The mission of the Diocesan Youth Conference in 2025 is to inspire the youth of the diocese to develop a personal relationship with God, to solidify their moral values and character, and to lead them to a deeper understanding of what it means to be rescued by our Savior, Jesus Christ.
