Welcome to DYC Talks

At the Diocesan Youth Conference, we host a variety of inspiring talks that focus on:

  • Youth Engagement
  • Faith Development
  • Leadership Skills
  • Community Involvement

Throughout the day on Saturday, you will have the flexibility to choose when you arrive. On Friday, there will be three rounds, each featuring adoration.

We encourage you to participate in these talks to enhance your understanding and engagement with the community.
