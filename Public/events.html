Upcoming Diocesan Events

Stay informed about the latest events organized by the Diocesan Youth Council. We invite all youth to join us!

Youth Leadership Conference

Date: November 12, 2024

Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Location: St. Peter's Church, 123 Church St, Hometown, NC

Description: Join us for a day of workshops, talks, and activities focused on developing leadership skills for young Catholics. There will be guest speakers, group discussions, and opportunities for prayer and fellowship.

Advent Retreat for Youth

Date: December 5-7, 2024

Location: Our Lady of the Rosary Retreat Center

Description: A weekend retreat focusing on preparing for the coming of Christ during the Advent season. Open to all youth in the diocese. Activities will include prayer, reflection, and outdoor recreation.

March for Life Pilgrimage

Date: January 20, 2025

Time: All day

Location: Washington, D.C.

Description: Join the Diocesan Youth Council in a pilgrimage to the annual March for Life in Washington, D.C. Youth from across the diocese are invited to come together and witness to the sanctity of life.
