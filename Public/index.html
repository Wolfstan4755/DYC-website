Mission Statement

“The mission of the Diocesan Youth Conference in 2025 is to

inspire the youth of the diocese to develop a personal relationship with God,

to solidify their moral values and character, and to lead them to a deeper

understanding of what it means to be rescued by our Savior, Jesus Christ.”

Conference Prayer

Heavenly Father, our Rescuer we cry out to You with hearts ablaze, desperate for Your saving grace.

You have rescued us time and time again—keep us from the grip of evil, persecution, and despair

that suffocates our lives. Shake us awake, O Lord, and remind us that only through You can we be rescued and

find eternal joy, freedom, and union. In this conference, let us rise as those who have been

rescued to become rescuers for others. Let us storm the gates of despair. Help us to feed the hungry,

shelter the homeless, accompany the lonely, and rescue the lost to find their path. Through the intercession

of St. Bernard Menthon, who knew the urgency of rescue, with his guidance, and the strength of

the Holy Spirit through Christ our Lord. Amen

Guidelines for Engagement

We encourage all members and followers to engage with us positively and respectfully. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Be Respectful: Treat others with kindness and respect in all interactions.
  • Stay on Topic: Keep discussions relevant to faith, youth engagement, and DYC activities.
  • Share Positivity: Share uplifting content that reflects our values and mission.
  • Report Concerns: If you see something inappropriate, report it to a DYC leader immediately.
